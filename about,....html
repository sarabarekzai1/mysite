House Duplex

A duplex home is a single residential building that contains two separate living units within one structure, each completely independent with its own entrance and facilities. Key Characteristics Two Units in One Building: The defining feature is the presence of two separate residences under a single roof. Configuration: The units can be side-by-side, sharing a common vertical wall (often called semi-detached homes in the UK), or stacked one above the other, sharing a floor/ceiling. Privacy and Independence: Each unit has its own private entrance, kitchen, and living areas, ensuring a high degree of privacy Rental Income: Ideal for generating passive income by renting out one unit.
